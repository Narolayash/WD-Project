North Indian Cuisine

North India is known for rich, aromatic, and flavorful dishes often cooked with ghee and spices:

  • Biryani: Afragrant rice dish made with aromatic spices and meat oe vegetables.
  • Butter Chicken: A creamy, tomato-based curry that's a favorite worldwide.
  • Panner Tikka: Marinated panner cubes gilled to perfection.

South Indian Cuisine

South India offers a wide array of dishes with a perfect blend of spices and tangy flavors:

  • Masala Dosa: A crispy rice crepe stuffed with spicy potato filling.
  • Idli and Sambar: Soft rice cakes served with a tangy lentil-based vegetables.
  • Chettinad Chicken: A fiery, flavorful chicken dish from Tamil Nadu.
